About Me

Aloha, I’m Ajmal Hassan I love to create beautiful things and it’s that love that drives me to put myself into every one of my creations, making it a work of art. There is a fine balance between aesthetics and functionality and to hit that middle ground, to make my design compliment the functionality is my forte. I am a freelance graphic designer but I love to code too so I took my love for both and I'm training myself to become a front-end developer as well as a UI/UX designer. I provide services like logo design, web design, book covers, posters, printing cards, invitations and so on. You name it. I'm curious and driven, a lethal combination that results in me having an unquenchable thirst for knowledge about the things that catches my fancy. I love to ransack the internet to get the resources I need to learn new design trends, languages, technologies and so on, so you can say that I'm a self learner.
